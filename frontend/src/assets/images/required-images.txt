REQUIRED IMAGES FOR SEO OPTIMIZATION
=====================================

Place the following images in the /public directory (not in assets):

1. og-image.jpg
   - Dimensions: 1200 x 630 pixels
   - Format: JPG
   - Purpose: Open Graph image for social media sharing (Facebook, LinkedIn)
   - Location: /public/og-image.jpg
   - Should include: Business logo, tagline, or product showcase

2. twitter-image.jpg
   - Dimensions: 1200 x 630 pixels (or 1200 x 600)
   - Format: JPG
   - Purpose: Twitter Card image
   - Location: /public/twitter-image.jpg
   - Should include: Business branding or key products

3. logo.png
   - Dimensions: At least 512 x 512 pixels (square)
   - Format: PNG (with transparency preferred)
   - Purpose: Logo for structured data and branding
   - Location: /public/logo.png
   - Should include: Company logo with transparent background

4. apple-touch-icon.png
   - Dimensions: 180 x 180 pixels
   - Format: PNG
   - Purpose: iOS home screen icon
   - Location: /public/apple-touch-icon.png
   - Should include: Company logo or icon

5. vite.svg (already exists)
   - Current favicon
   - Location: /public/vite.svg
